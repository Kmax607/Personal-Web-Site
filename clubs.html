Curling Club

Open to all students of Penn State University, Curling at PSU aims to grow the sport and create lifelong curlers. The Penn State Curling Club provides a fun and engaging environment for students to learn and compete in the sport. We offer a variety of programs and activities, including a student league for all skill levels, a travel team competing in the USA Curling College National Championship, and open "learn-to-curl" sessions for the public. Joining the Penn State Curling Club is a great way to get involved in a new sport, meet new people, and have fun.

Outdoors Club

The Penn State Outing Club (PSOC) is an entirely student-run organization dedicated to experiencing the outdoors. Founded in 1920, PSOC organizes trips across Pennsylvania and the east coast. If you love hiking, backpacking, and overnight trips outdoors, PSOC is the club for you. Join PSOC to gain opportunities to learn about the great outdoors and travel to various locations.
